The Objective

The goal was to increase brand visibility and generate qualified leads for Vistamax Consultants. I needed to lower the Cost Per Lead (CPL) while simultaneously building long-term organic traffic through content.

Strategic Execution

  • A/B Testing: Designed variations of ad creatives, finding that high-contrast visuals performed 30% better than generic stock photos.
  • SEO Strategy: Identified low-competition keywords and optimized blog content to rank for "Student Visa Consultants."
  • Content Calendar: Maintained a consistent multi-channel schedule to nurture leads at the "Consideration" stage of the funnel.
